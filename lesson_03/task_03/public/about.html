A Brief History of Cats

Cats have been living alongside humans for thousands of years. Domestication of cats is believed to have started around 4,000 years ago in ancient Egypt, where they were revered and even worshipped as gods. The ancient Egyptians kept cats as companions, but they also valued them for their ability to protect grain stores from mice and rats.

Over time, cats spread across the world, accompanying travelers and traders on their journeys. In the Middle Ages, cats were associated with superstitions, and some even believed they had magical powers. Despite this, they continued to be valuable companions in homes, helping to control pests.

Today, cats are one of the most popular pets worldwide. Known for their independent yet affectionate nature, they have found a special place in the hearts of millions. Whether lounging in a sunny spot or chasing after a toy, cats bring joy and comfort to their owners.
